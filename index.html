01 About

How
I
Think.

I’m a brand and marketing professional with experience across F&B, FMCG, fashion, wellness, and lifestyle. I’ve worked on brand strategy, campaigns, retail experiences, social, influencer, and product launches — often across all of these at once, for the same brand.

At Please See//, I own accounts end-to-end — writing the brief, guiding the team, managing the client, and seeing the work through to launch. Some of my favourite moments have been when the work we delivered opened up a conversation the brand wasn’t expecting to have — and that’s usually where the most interesting work begins. My instinct is always to go one level deeper than the brief — to understand what the brand actually needs, not just what’s been asked for.
